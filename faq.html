AqiNode is a startup — not a solo freelancer. We operate as a small but focused team, bringing together design, engineering, and strategy under one roof. This means more accountability, better communication, and consistent quality throughout your project.

We work with startups, early-stage founders, growing businesses, and organizations looking to digitize or automate. Whether you have a rough idea or a detailed spec, we can help you build it.

Process

Our typical process is: (1) Discovery call to understand your needs, (2) Scoping and proposal, (3) Design and architecture, (4) Development with regular check-ins, (5) Testing & QA, (6) Deployment, (7) Handoff + optional ongoing support. We keep you in the loop at every stage.

Timelines depend heavily on project scope. A simple web app or landing product could take 2–4 weeks. A full SaaS platform with AI features typically takes 6–12 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline estimate during scoping. We don't rush — we ship right.

We typically work with fixed-price project scopes for well-defined projects, and time-and-materials (hourly/weekly retainer) for ongoing or exploratory work. After a discovery conversation, we'll recommend the model that makes the most sense for your situation.
